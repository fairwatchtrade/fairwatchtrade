*For V1 test build — standalone shareable quiz, no inventory matching yet*

**1. What's on the dial, or what's inside?**
The single most important question. Status buyer = the dial (recognizable). Collector = what's inside (movement, complication, finishing nobody sees but you).

**2. Would you rather have 100 people notice your watch, or 1 person who truly understands it?**

**3. Do you care if people recognize your watch?**
Follow-up line: *"The watches with the deepest collector communities are often completely unrecognizable to most people. That invisibility is actually the point."*

**4. Do you prefer knowing your watch needs daily attention, or forgetting it exists?**
Manual wind vs. automatic — this is about MECHANISM PHILOSOPHY.

**5. Do you like feeling real weight and presence on your wrist, or do you prefer something so light you forget it's there?**
Physical wearing comfort — distinct from Question 4. Movement philosophy and physical comfort preference are NOT the same axis, even though they sound similar.

**6. Bold statement, or quiet confidence?**
